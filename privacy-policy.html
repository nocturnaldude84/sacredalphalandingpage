Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Sacred Alpha ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website sacredalpha.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. We have written it to be read — not to be buried.

Information We Collect

Information you give us directly

When you use the contact form on our site, you provide your name and email address. We use this information solely to respond to your message. We do not add you to any mailing list, share your details with third parties, or use your contact information for any purpose beyond the conversation you initiated.

Information collected automatically

When you visit sacredalpha.com, certain information is collected automatically through Google Analytics. This includes your approximate geographic location (country and city level), the device and browser you are using, the pages you visit and how long you spend on them, and how you arrived at the site (for example, via a search engine or a direct link).

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We cannot identify you individually from Google Analytics data, and we do not attempt to. We use it solely to understand how the site is being used so we can improve it.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Sacred Alpha uses cookies in the following ways:

  • Google Analytics cookies — these track anonymous usage data as described above. They are set by Google and governed by Google's own privacy policy.
  • Insight Timer embed cookies — pages containing an embedded Insight Timer player may set cookies from the Insight Timer platform. These are governed by Insight Timer's privacy policy.
  • No advertising or tracking cookies — Sacred Alpha does not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or any cookies designed to track you across other websites.

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using this site.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use sacredalpha.com. Google Analytics collects data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and general geographic location. This data is processed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

If you wish to opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, Google provides a browser add-on for this purpose at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Third-Party Links and Embeds

Sacred Alpha contains links to and embedded content from third-party platforms including Insight Timer, Instagram, and YouTube. When you interact with embedded content or follow a link to one of these platforms, you leave the Sacred Alpha site and that platform's own privacy policy applies. We have no control over, and accept no responsibility for, the privacy practices of third-party sites.

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Contact form submissions are received directly via email. We do not operate a database of user information, and we do not store personal data beyond what is necessary to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes — ever.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data under applicable law — including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any information we hold about you. If you submitted a contact form and wish to have that information removed, or if you have any question about how your data is handled, contact us at:

sacredalpha.official@gmail.com

We will respond to any such request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Sacred Alpha is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our site, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
